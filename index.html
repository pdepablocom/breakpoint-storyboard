Production Notes

Color Palette

White #FFFFFF (primary bg)
#F5F5F5 (light scenes)
#0A0A0A (dark accents)
#CCFF00 (green accents)

Typography

Aeonik Pro Medium (500) — primary font for all text. Inter as fallback. -0.02em letter-spacing on headlines. Clean, confident weight throughout.

Icons

Remix Icon library. Five color variants per brand guide. Custom sidebar icons for CRM screenshots. Max icon container radius: 12px.

Border Radius

Max 12px (brand rule). Small: 4px, Medium: 8px (default), Large: 12px. Pill/tags only: 9999px. No rounded corners beyond 12px.

Pacing & Backgrounds

11 of 15 scenes use white/light backgrounds. Dark scenes reserved for: hook stats (01), brand reveal (04), results (13), and CTA (15). Creates elegant rhythm.

Aspect Ratio

1920×1080 (16:9) for web/presentation. Consider 1080×1920 vertical cut for Instagram Reels, TikTok, LinkedIn Stories.
